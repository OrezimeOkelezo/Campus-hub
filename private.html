Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, matriculation number, and profile picture when you create an account on Student Connect.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to personalize your experience, allow you to post content, interact with other students, and track your "Points" within the community system.

3. Storage and Security

We use Supabase for secure data and image storage. While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Content Sharing

Information you post (comments, posts, shared ebooks) is visible to other registered users of the platform. Please be mindful of what you share publicly.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage and session tokens to keep you logged in and remember your preferences across different pages of the app.

6. Your Rights

You can update your profile information at any time through the Settings page. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact support.
